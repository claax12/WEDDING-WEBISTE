OUR LOVE STORY

Eric and Cory met at Carlton Oaks Golf Course in the spring of 2024 and felt an immediate connection—one sparked by laughter, honesty, and a sense of familiarity neither could ignore. Both had weathered loss and change, yet each brought to the other a lightness and comfort that felt effortless, as if they had been waiting to meet all along.

What began with casual conversations, late-night messages, and shared smiles soon became something deeper. Their friendship grew into a love built on respect, kindness, and the quiet joy of simply being together—proof that even after life’s hardest chapters, the heart still knows how to begin again.

This December, Eric and Cory invite you to join them in celebrating their marriage and the beautiful life they are creating together—a reminder that love doesn’t always come early, but it always arrives right on time.
